# TaskFlow Pricing Plans

TaskFlow offers four plans to fit teams of all sizes. All paid plans include
a 14-day free trial — no credit card required.

## Free
**$0 / user / month**

Ideal for individuals and very small teams getting started.

- Up to 3 active projects
- Unlimited tasks
- Up to 2 team members
- 10 MB file attachments (per file)
- Kanban and List views only
- Community support only

## Pro
**$12 / user / month (billed annually) or $15 monthly**

The most popular plan for growing teams.

- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited team members
- 100 MB file attachments (per file)
- All views (Kanban, List, Calendar, Timeline)
- Custom labels and priorities
- Slack, Google Drive, and Zapier integrations
- Email support (24-hour response time during business hours)

## Business
**$24 / user / month (billed annually) or $29 monthly**

For teams that need advanced controls and integrations.

- Everything in Pro
- Private projects
- Advanced permissions and roles
- Custom saved views
- GitHub, Jira, and webhook integrations
- SSO via Google Workspace
- Audit log (90 days retention)
- Priority email support (4-hour response time during business hours)

## Enterprise
**Custom pricing — contact sales**

For organizations with compliance, security, or scale requirements.

- Everything in Business
- SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning
- Unlimited audit log retention
- Data residency options (EU, US)
- Dedicated customer success manager
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- 24/7 email support and phone support on request

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I change plans later?**
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**.
Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

**Do you offer discounts?**
We offer 20% off for annual billing, 30% off for nonprofits, and free Pro
accounts for accredited students and educators. Email
support@taskflow.example.com to apply.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
All major credit cards and PayPal for Pro plans. Business and Enterprise
plans can also pay by bank transfer on annual billing.

**What currency is billing in?**
Billing is in USD by default. EU customers can opt for EUR billing from
**Settings → Billing → Currency**.

**What happens when the trial ends?**
When your 14-day trial ends, your workspace automatically moves to the Free
plan unless you add a payment method. No data is deleted.

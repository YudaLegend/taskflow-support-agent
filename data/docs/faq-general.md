# Frequently Asked Questions

General questions about TaskFlow. For plan-specific or feature-specific
questions, see the related docs.

## About TaskFlow

**What is TaskFlow?**
TaskFlow is a web-based project management tool for teams. It helps you
plan, organize, and track work across projects, tasks, and deadlines.

**Who makes TaskFlow?**
TaskFlow is made by TaskFlow S.L., a company founded in 2021 and
headquartered in Barcelona, Spain.

**What platforms does TaskFlow run on?**
TaskFlow is primarily a web application that works in any modern browser.
We also offer native iOS and Android apps. There is no desktop app —
we recommend using the web version in a browser tab.

## Accounts and getting started

**Is TaskFlow free?**
Yes. We offer a Free plan forever with up to 3 projects and 2 team members.
Paid plans start at $12 per user per month.

**Do I need a credit card to sign up?**
No. You can sign up and use the Free plan without entering any payment
information. You only need a card if you upgrade to a paid plan.

**Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
Yes. All paid plans include a 14-day free trial, and no credit card is
required to start the trial.

**Can I import my data from another tool?**
Yes. TaskFlow supports importing from CSV, JSON, Trello, and Asana. Go to
**Settings → Data → Import** to get started. Workspace admins only.

## Data and privacy

**Where is my data stored?**
Data is stored in AWS data centers in the EU by default. Enterprise
customers can choose US data residency.

**Is my data backed up?**
Yes. We back up all data every 6 hours with 30 days of retention. You can
also export your data at any time.

**Who can see my projects?**
Only members you explicitly invite. Projects are private to the workspace
by default. On Business and Enterprise plans, projects can also be made
private so only selected members within the workspace can see them.

**Is TaskFlow GDPR compliant?**
Yes. We're a Spanish company and GDPR compliance is built in. You can
request a data export or deletion at any time.

## Using TaskFlow

**How many projects can I have?**
Free plans are limited to 3 active projects. All paid plans are unlimited.

**How many tasks can a project have?**
Unlimited, on all plans.

**Can I use TaskFlow offline?**
The mobile apps support offline mode. The web app requires an internet
connection, but we're exploring offline support for the web as well.

**Can I have multiple workspaces?**
Yes. A single account can be a member of unlimited workspaces. Each
workspace has its own projects, members, and billing.

## Support

**How do I contact support?**
Email support@taskflow.example.com. Response times depend on your plan:

- **Free** — community forum only
- **Pro** — within 24 hours (business hours)
- **Business** — within 4 hours (business hours)
- **Enterprise** — 24/7 with dedicated support

Business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 18:00 CET.

**Is there a phone number?**
Phone support is available only for Enterprise customers.

**Where do I report a bug?**
Email support@taskflow.example.com with "Bug report" in the subject, or
file a report at status.taskflow.example.com.

**Where can I see if TaskFlow is down?**
Visit status.taskflow.example.com for real-time system status and
historical uptime.

## Common requests

**Can you add feature X?**
Maybe. We keep a public roadmap at taskflow.example.com/roadmap where you
can vote on feature requests. Popular requests are prioritized.

**Can I get a custom integration built?**
Enterprise customers can discuss custom integrations with their customer
success manager. Other customers should use Zapier or the API.

**Do you offer discounts?**
Yes:

- 20% off for annual billing (all paid plans)
- 30% off for nonprofits and registered charities
- Free Pro accounts for accredited students and educators

Contact support@taskflow.example.com to apply.

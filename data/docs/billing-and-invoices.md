# Billing and Invoices

All billing for TaskFlow is managed from **Settings → Billing** in the web
app. Only workspace admins can see or change billing information.

## Billing cycles

TaskFlow offers two billing cycles:

- **Monthly** — charged on the same day each month
- **Annual** — charged once per year, with a 20% discount compared to
  monthly

You can switch between cycles at any time. Switching from monthly to annual
prorates the unused portion of your current month toward the annual charge.
Switching from annual to monthly takes effect at your next renewal date.

## Viewing invoices

Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices** to see all past invoices. You can:

- **Download** any invoice as a PDF
- **Email** any invoice to your accounting team
- **Update** the billing email address that receives new invoices
- **Add** company name, tax ID (VAT, GST, etc.), and billing address

All invoices include line items, taxes, and the invoice number required for
accounting.

## Adding or changing payment methods

Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment methods** to add a new card or payment
method. You can store multiple payment methods and choose which is primary.

Supported payment methods:

- **Credit card** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **PayPal** — available on Pro, Business, Enterprise plans
- **Bank transfer** — available on Business and Enterprise plans with annual
  billing only
- **SEPA direct debit** — available for EU customers on annual billing

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, we'll retry it automatically up to 3 times over 7 days.
You'll receive an email after each failed attempt. During this grace period
your workspace continues to work normally.

If all retries fail, your workspace is moved to the Free plan and you'll
lose access to paid features. Your data is never deleted — re-adding a valid
payment method restores full access immediately.

## Refunds

We offer refunds under these conditions:

- **Annual plans** — full refund within 30 days of purchase, prorated
  refunds thereafter on request
- **Monthly plans** — refunds available only for accidental double-charges
  or billing errors
- **Enterprise plans** — refund terms are defined in your contract

To request a refund, email support@taskflow.example.com with your invoice
number.

## Taxes

TaskFlow collects applicable taxes (VAT, GST, sales tax) based on your
billing address and tax ID.

- **EU customers** — VAT is charged unless a valid EU VAT number is provided
- **US customers** — sales tax is charged in states where TaskFlow has tax
  nexus
- **Other countries** — see **Settings → Billing → Tax info** for your
  specific situation

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I get an invoice for a future date?**
No. Invoices are only generated when charges are actually made.

**Why was I charged a different amount than expected?**
Most differences are due to user count changes (adding or removing team
members mid-cycle), proration, or taxes. The invoice PDF shows a line-item
breakdown.

**Can I pay in my local currency?**
USD and EUR are supported directly. Other currencies are converted by your
card issuer at their exchange rate.

**Who should I contact about billing questions?**
Email support@taskflow.example.com with the subject "Billing" and include
your workspace name and invoice number if relevant.
